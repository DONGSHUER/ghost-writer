Chinese overseas students outsource their homework to paid writers, giving rise to a well-formed ghost-writing industry

by Cai Zhiling, Chen Wantong, Dong Shuer, and Wang Heyu

READ MORE

These days, more and more Chinese international students tend to seek help from ghost-writers to relieve their academic stress, making this business blossoming into a well-formed industry. However, the prosperity of ghost-writing can cause a lot of problems legally and academically.



Ghost-writer is by your side

How to write an excellent paper and assignments is one of the compulsory courses for students in their entire academic life. Some people achieve it easily, while others suffer a lot from it.

Given limited time resources in the study, the student may turn to the Internet and seek a capable assistant. We are used to seeing ads pop up when we go onto sites because the social platforms believe you are interested in; Students who have searched for "write college papers" on the Internet have become the prey of the "essay mills" for the same reason.

StudyGate official website


Ghost-writing is a unique delivery service by client-entrusted companies or self-organized teams for writing academic papers and assignments. Customers only need to provide information about the subject's requirements and the expected completion time of the task. By completing academic work based on pre-conditions, the ghost-writers could take money but none of the credit for the produced work.


It is born in college

The number of students pursuing study overseas is growing snowballing in China. Faced with the learning pressures, some Chinese students try to avoid studying work and ask for outside help. The ghost-writing services came next, enabling the set-up of a number of websites and social media accounts.

Joey, a ghost-writer with abroad studying experience in America, shared a scenario when he studied abroad: the on-campus students who chose to have ghost-writing services were too lazy to finish the homework but somehow hoping to get satisfactory marks. As the BBC reported, ghost-writers gradually developed from assisting students to complete homework with paid money or food.

Prosperously developing industry with crafted sales strategies

Demands for ghost-writing will never be sufficient as the non-stopping trend of study abroad in China. There are two kinds of 'professional' service providers occupying the ghost-writing market, including the agents and self-organized teams. Running ghost-writing as an official business, they have built their network delicately with three employee types: customer service staff, the recruiter, and ghost-writers.

It is easy for individuals to start writing services. You could promote yourself on social platforms as long as you have the required skills. For example, there is an "international student" tag on Weibo. The students who need service will write down their requirements, and the writers will put on their transcripts. If they are satisfied with each other, the transaction carries out. Subsequently, the individuals as "shadow scholars" can organize to form a writing agent or team. Joey said that he couldn't find a job when he graduated, and then he invited a few friends to set up a ghost-writing organization.

The agencies have their name cards recommended by students on WeChat, and the Instagram accounts just following target people and giving examples of how they would do your work.

The article writing price ranges from 600 to 800 yuan per thousand words, and the programming services charge depends on difficulty varying from fifty to thousands. Most of the transactions finish through online payment methods, including PayPal, Visa, Master, and WeChat.


Case Study: Student League and Simpletense

Student League and Simpletense are well-organized enterprises in ghost-writing. Among them, Simpletense is a company that mainly serves to write English papers with its sub-company working on science subjects, while Student League focuses explicitly on science and finance.

Simpletense advertises on their official website that they have native language writers and North American universities' admissions officers as a gimmick to attract students and writers. We reflected on the fact after asking to be one of the essay-writers and got refused for not being a native speaker. Simpletense claims to be an educational institution officially registered in America.

The sub-company — StudyGate even got seed funding of US$ 350K in 2017.

Risks that consumers may suffer in buying copywriting works

The lack of standardization in the industry of ghost-writing can cause a lot of problems for the consumers, among which the work quality cannot be guaranteed is the biggest one. Some ghost-writers choose to simply offer students essays of poor quality or offer a paper to several students for cost-saving, either of which will leave the students a fail grade. Nevertheless, some self-claimed ghost-writers even cheat students only for the deposit, and no essays will be given after the students get blacklisted by the con man.

Rae's sharing post on Weibo

Rae, a Chinese 21-year-old accounting student, studying in Europe, got trolled in a crucial exam by a ghost-writing agency called essay master this September. Rae got acquainted with this agency's staff in a WeChat group for students abroad to buy return tickets. Later, to get a higher grade in a vital financing test, Rae decided to make her first attempt at purchasing the ghost-writing service. The deal was finally made at the price of 1300 yuan.

However, to Rae's surprise and anger, after 3 hours' exam time, the agency just gave her an answer for a 9-point question out of the 100-point paper. The agency apologized that they don't know their ghost-writer cannot handle this test, but they only agreed to return half of the price." I won't buy any ghost-writing works anymore." said Rae. After finding the fraud, Rae could do nothing but scolding the agency to express her dissatisfaction.

Normally, the rights and interests of consumers of ghost-writing are not protected by the law. According to the Copyright Law of the People's Republic of China, the right of the person of the work cannot be transferred to anybody else. Meanwhile, the deal of ghost-writing also violates the Regulations of the People's Republic of China on Academic Degrees. Therefore, the contracts between consumers and ghost-writers are invalid, which means any loss under these contracts will not be saved.


The subsequent processing of ghost-writing in universities

Although many students get used to using ghost-writing in their academic lives, the behavior of using ghost-writing has always suffered a lot of controversies about justice.

"I don't think it's fair towards other students." Nick was a Ukrainian student majoring in business information management at the University of California, Irvine. He held firm opposition views towards ghost-writing. Nick explained:" Because you know, for many reasons, other students might not be able to have access to such a resource, they might not be able to afford it. I see how this can affect my peers and my friends."

Legally, ghost writing is strictly prohibited. According to Prevention and treatment of academic misconduct in Colleges and Universities published in 2016, anyone who get involved in the transactions of academic essays, including both ghostwriters and consumers, will be published. For the students, their degrees will be canceled and they may lose their qualifications to apply for the degree.

However, universities meet with some difficulties in distinguishing the pieces written by ghost-writers. As Mr. Wang, an assistant professor at Hong Kong Baptist University, suggested that universities' primary system to check students' works, Turnitin, can only conduct two types of checking, intragroup checking and public database checking. Mr. Wang stated that both ways could do little in picking out ghost-writing ones because they can only check similarities of different works." Only if the ghost-writers produce something that they're here before, the Turnitin system can report its high similarity."

And even if the pieces have been distinguished by the system and teachers, those found guilty of academic misconduct can also escape from punishments with some agencies' assistance. An agency called FindingSchool gave a case of helping students out of this dilemma. Firstly, they helped the student write a letter of appeal to the professor. Then the agency held rehearsals of hearings for the student many times. Finally, the student got mitigation successfully, with the suspension punishment lessened to probation.
